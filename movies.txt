Movie: Ghajini (2008)
Director: A.R. Murugadoss
Lead Actor: Aamir Khan
Lead Actress: Asin
Villain: Ghajini Dharmatma (played by Pradeep Rawat)

Story:
Sanjay Singhania is a wealthy businessman who suffers from short-term memory loss after a violent attack.
He uses tattoos, photographs, and notes to track information as he seeks revenge against Ghajini Dharmatma,
the man who murdered his fiancée Kalpana Shetty.

Who Dies:
Kalpana Shetty is killed by Ghajini Dharmatma.

Ending:
Sanjay finally kills Ghajini Dharmatma and later resumes his life, honoring Kalpana’s memory.

Influences:
Ghajini is a remake of the Tamil film Ghajini (2005).
The film was inspired by the Hollywood movie Memento (2000).
It is not an official copy of Memento.

Box Office Collection:
₹411 crore worldwide
